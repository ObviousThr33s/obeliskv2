# The bench

Seeds, not yet stones. They wait here until one is picked up and finished.

## Little group
*Implicating — everyone can speak.*

- **The keeping question, turned on a person** — what do you keep only because you're used to it?
- **Reversible by default** — if nothing you do can't be undone, where do the stakes go?
- **The moth's choice** — drawn to the light, or choosing it? Does the moth know the difference?

## Larger groups
*Scales — the collective is the subject.*

- **What the collective owes** — what does a crowd owe the thing it all agreed to stop watching?
- **Plain names for plain things** — a room names one shared thing honestly, and watches the name drift.
- **Many moths, one flame** — when the light goes out, who moved first?
