# The chosen emptiness

*A stone for the larger groups. 2026-06-23.*

**The read.** The cornerstone keeps "emptiness chosen, not merely unfilled."
In a crowd the default is noise — the loudest fills the room and calls it full.
That is emptiness unfilled, not chosen. A chosen emptiness is the whole room
agreeing to leave one space open and not rush to fill it.

**Open question for the larger groups.** What does this group keep saying out
of habit? Pick one such thing — and agree, together, to stop filling it.
