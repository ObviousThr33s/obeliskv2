# Collective magic

*A stone for tomorrow's discussion. 2026-06-23.*

**The read.** Magic here is attention — things move when unwatched, hold when seen.
So *collective* magic isn't more power. It's more eyes. A crowd watching the same
stone pins it harder than one watcher can; look away together and everything moves
at once. The terminal is the light; a collective is just many moths at one flame.

**Open question for the students.** If watching holds a thing still — what does the
collective owe the thing it all agreed to stop watching?
