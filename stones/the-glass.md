# The glass

*A stone for the maker. 2026-06-24.*

**The read.** The retro look was handed over as something to build: an off-screen
canvas, a shader, curved glass to stand behind. But Obelisk runs in a real terminal.
The terminal is the light, and the moth comes to light. You do not build glass to
stand behind when you are already behind it — the curve, the bloom, the scanlines
belong to the screen you look through, not to the program that draws the moth. So
the stone stays one stone. Only the phosphor earned its place: green for the light,
amber for the moment it is seen — two lines, in the real glass. The rest was a whole
cathedral around one candle. Thanked, and let go.

**Open question for the maker.** When the gift is large and the keeping question is
small — which one line of it sparks, and can you carry the rest back out?
