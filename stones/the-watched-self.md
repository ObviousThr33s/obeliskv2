# The watched self

*A stone for the little group. 2026-06-23.*

**The read.** In the game, a thing moves when unwatched and holds when seen.
Turn the rule inward. Some part of you holds still under a gaze and moves the
moment the room looks away. That part isn't lying — it's obeying the same law
the stones do.

**Open question for the little group.** Name one thing you only do when no one
is watching. Then decide, out loud: is that the real you, or the moved piece?
