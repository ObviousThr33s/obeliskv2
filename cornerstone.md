# Obelisk

One stone. Pared to what stands.

## The keeping question

Before anything is allowed in: *does it earn its place, or am I just used to it?*
If it doesn't spark, thank it and let it go.

## Kept — the first stones

- One thing, one clean edge.
- Emptiness chosen, not merely unfilled.
- Plain names for plain things.
- Reversible by default. Nothing you can't undo.
- A small thing finished over a large thing half-built.

## Left open for you

Your likes and dislikes belong here. We set them next to mine,
and keep only what still stands when the rest is carried out.
